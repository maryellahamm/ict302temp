For our walkthrough assignments, our team will be exprected to crete two pages on WordPress where we provide an step-by-step guide to accomplishing a particular task in WordPress. The specific task will be delegated by Dr. Greenhalgh. The same workflow is expected to be followed as we complete this assignment. 
For submission, our group will upload two links for two complete walkthroughs that describe our efforts throughout the semester. 
*The requirements for this assignment include:*
- containing at least 500 words of original content\
- use of at least two markup elements
- identification of who played what role and steps taken
- discussion of decision making and obstacles encountered
 For each of the walkthroughs:
 -- the group should give multiple possibilites for how to do something and describe important advice
 -- draw from course concepts and links to resources
