In this assignment, our group will create two pages on Wordpress where resources will be listed and linked where you can find helpful resources to a particular skill or topic related to content managment. A workflow will be followed to create these blog posts. The requirements of this assignment include using 500 words of original content. The group must use two markup elements that appear on the screen. Our page must include media and identify who played what role an lists the specific steps each person took. We will need to discuss how decisions were made and communicated amongst the group. We must cite 10 resources for our work; these sources must be from reputable sites and describe the qualifications of the sources.
